The XLink namespace is declared at the top of the document (xmlns:xlink="http://www.w3.org/1999/xlink") The xlink:type="simple" creates a simple "HTML-like" link The xlink:href attribute specifies the URL to link to (in this case - an image) The xlink:show="new" specifies that the link should open in a new window
